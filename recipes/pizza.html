Delicious homemade pizza recipe

Description

This classic Neapolitan-style Margherita pizza is simple, fresh, and satisfying. A thin, slightly chewy crust is topped with a bright tomato sauce, fresh mozzarella, and fragrant basil leaves — finished with a drizzle of extra-virgin olive oil.

It’s perfect for a quick weeknight dinner or for impressing friends. Use the best quality tomatoes and mozzarella you can find — the recipe's success depends on fresh ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 500 g (about 4 cups) pizza dough (store-bought or homemade, enough for 2 medium pizzas)
  • 1 cup (240 g) canned San Marzano tomatoes, crushed (or good-quality pizza sauce)
  • 250 g fresh mozzarella, torn into pieces
  • Fresh basil leaves, a handful
  • 2 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt (plus extra for seasoning)
  • Flour or semolina for dusting
