Delicious homemade tomato soup recipe

Description

This classic tomato soup is rich, smooth, and comforting — perfect for cool evenings or a light lunch. Made with ripe tomatoes, aromatic vegetables, and a touch of cream, it balances sweetness and tang beautifully. Serve it with crusty bread, grilled cheese, or fresh herbs for a cozy, homemade meal.

You can use fresh tomatoes when in season, or canned tomatoes for a quick and flavorful year-round option. The recipe takes under an hour and is easy enough for beginners.

Ingredients

  • 500 g (about 4 cups) ripe tomatoes, chopped (or 1 can of good-quality crushed tomatoes)
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 cup (240 ml) vegetable broth
  • 1/2 cup (120 ml) heavy cream (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish
and fragrant basil leaves — finished with a drizzle of extra-virgin olive oil.

Instructions

  1. Sauté the aromatics: Heat olive oil or butter in a large pot over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and carrots. Cook for 5–7 minutes, stirring occasionally, until softened and slightly golden.
  2. Add garlic: Stir in the minced garlic and cook for another 1–2 minutes until fragrant.
  3. Add tomatoes: Add the chopped tomatoes (or canned tomatoes) and vegetable broth. Bring to a simmer and cook for 20–25 minutes, until the tomatoes are soft and the flavors meld.
  4. Blend: Use an immersion blender to puree the soup until smooth. (Alternatively, carefully transfer to a blender in batches.)
  5. Add cream: If using, stir in the heavy cream and season with salt and pepper to taste. Heat gently until warmed through.
  6. Serve: Ladle the soup into bowls and garnish with fresh basil leaves. Enjoy with crusty bread or grilled cheese!
